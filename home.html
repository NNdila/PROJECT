Braised Beef Short Ribs

The finest cut of beef for slow cooking is short ribs! These Braised meat Short Ribs are the most tender and succulent fall-apart meat you have ever had; at fine dining establishments, you would pay top price for a platter.

Tiramisu is an elegant and rich layered Italian dessert made with delicate ladyfinger cookies, espresso or instant espresso, mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, Marsala wine, rum and cocoa powder.
