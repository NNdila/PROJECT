About Us


Welcome to What's Cookin' with Nicole, your ultimate destination for culinary inspiration, delicious recipes, and practical cooking tips!

Our Mission


At What's Cookin' with Nicole, our mission is to empower home cooks of all skill levels to explore their culinary creativity, expand their repertoire, and discover the joy of cooking. We believe that cooking should be enjoyable, accessible, and rewarding for everyone, and we're here to guide you on your culinary journey.


Who We Are


What's Cookin' with Nicole was founded by Nicole, a passionate home cook with a love for food and a desire to share her knowledge and enthusiasm with others. Her deputy, Megatron the cat has a refined and judgemental taste palate, that has aided in her training. With their years of experience experimenting in the kitchen and a deep appreciation for diverse cuisines and flavors, the dream team is dedicated to helping others develop their culinary skills and confidence.


What We Offer


On What's Cookin' with Nicole, you'll find a wide range of mouthwatering recipes, from quick and easy weeknight dinners to impressive gourmet meals for special occasions. Whether you're looking for comforting classics, healthy options, international cuisines, or creative twists on traditional dishes, we've got you covered.
In addition to our extensive collection of recipes, we provide helpful cooking tips, techniques, and kitchen hacks to make your cooking experience more enjoyable and successful. From ingredient substitutions to time-saving tricks, we're here to answer your questions and offer expert advice.
