Braised Beef Short Ribs



These Braised meat Short Ribs are the most tender and succulent fall-apart meat you'll ever have!


Ingredients


5 - 6 beef short ribs , 300-400g/10-14oz each (Note 1)
1.5 tsp each salt and pepper
2 tbsp olive oil
3 garlic cloves , crushed
1 large onion , chopped
celery ribs , chopped
2 carrots , chopped
2 tbsp tomato paste
2 cups (500ml) dry red wine
2 cups (500ml) beef stock/broth, low sodium
2 sprigs thyme (optional)
2 bay leaves


Instructions


Preheat oven to 160°C/325°F.
Sprinkle beef all over with salt and pepper.
Heat oil in a large ovenproof pot over high heat. Add half the ribs and brown aggressively all over (~5 - 7 min in total). Remove and repeat with remaining ribs, then remove.
Turn heat down to medium. Add onion and garlic into the same pot and cook for 2 minutes.
Add carrot and celery, cook for 5 minutes until carrot is softened and sweet.
Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.
Add wine, broth, thyme and bay leaves. Stir until tomato paste is dissolved.
Return beef into liquid, arranging them so they are submerged.
Cover with lid and transfer to oven for 3 hours, or until the meat can easily be pried apart with forks.
Remove beef carefully, keeping the meat on the bone.
Cover to keep warm.
Strain all liquid in the pot, pressing juices out of the onion, carrot etc. (Optional - can skip if you don't mind chunky sauce) Return sauce into pot, bring to simmer and stir.
Adjust as necessary - simmer to reduce/thicken, add water to thin, season with salt and pepper if needed.
Place beef on serving plate, spoon over sauce. Serve!

Substitutes


Non alcoholic sub: Use non alcoholic wine OR use 0.5 extra cup of beef broth (must be low sodium), 1.5 cups water, 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce in place of 2 cups wine.

Sautéed Mushrooms



The standard garnish for any kind of steak is these sautéed mushrooms.


Ingredients


Mushrooms
Olive Oil
Butter
Garlic
Soy Sauce
Balsamic Vinegar
Fresh Thyme


Instructions


Clean and cut the mushrooms.
Sauté with butter and oil.
Saute mushrooms until they are tender and the liquid cooks away. Stir in the soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
Add the fresh herbs. ENJOY!


Substitutions

Fresh parsley or rosemary are also excellent herbs for sautéed mushrooms.


Tips!

To Freeze. You can freeze sautéed mushrooms in an airtight freezer-safe storage container for up to 3 months. Let thaw overnight in the refrigerator before reheating.
To Store. Refrigerate mushrooms in an airtight storage container for up to 5 days.


Tiramisu


Tiramisu is an elegant and rich, no-bake layered Italian dessert


Ingredients


1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
8 ounce container mascarpone cheese ,room temperature
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups cold espresso
3 Tablespoons coffee flavored liqueur ,optional (Kahlua or DaVinci brands)
1 package Lady Fingers ,Savoiardi brand can be found in the cookie aisle at your local grocery store, or online
Cocoa powder for dusting the top

Instructions


Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks.
Add the espresso and liqueur (if using) to a shallow bowl and dip the lady fingers on both sides (don’t let them soak–just a quick dip!)
Smooth a layer of the mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of the lady fingers.
Repeat. Add another layer of lady fingers (dipped in coffee and liqueur) and another layer of cheese mixture. Dust with cocoa powder.

Substitutions


Alcohol free: Leave it out completely.
Alcohol variations: use dark rum, brandy, marsala wine.
Espresso substitutions: coffee, decaf


Tips!

Tiramisu is even better when made in advance, allowing the flavors to blend! It will keep in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days.
If frozen,Thaw in the refrigerator overnight and dust with cocoa powder a few hours before serving.
